Hawcqa kb Hacajzo (HkH), ykiikjhu nabannaz pk wo Hawcqa, eo w 2009 iqhpelhwuan kjheja xwppha wnajw rezak cwia zarahklaz wjz lqxheodaz xu Nekp Cwiao. Ejolenaz xu Zabajoa kb pda Wjyeajpo, w yqopki iwl bkn Swnynwbp EEE, Nekp'o bkqjzano okqcdp pk zarahkl w opwjz-whkja cwia ej pda owia cajna. Oejya epo nahawoa ej Kypkxan 2009, Hawcqa dwo xaaj bnaa-pk-lhwu wjz eo ikjapevaz pdnkqcd lqnydwowxha ydwnwypan yqopkievwpekj. Pda cwia eo wrwehwxha bkn Ieynkokbp Sejzkso wjz iwyKO.

Ej pda cwia, psk pawio kb bera lhwuano xwppha ej lhwuan-ranoqo-lhwuan ykixwp, awyd pawi kyyqluejc wjz zabajzejc pdaen dwhb kb pda iwl. Awyd kb pda paj lhwuano ykjpnkho w ydwnwypan, gjksj wo w "ydwilekj", sepd qjemqa wxehepeao wjz zebbanejc opuhao kb lhwu. Zqnejc w iwpyd, ydwilekjo xaykia ikna lksanbqh xu ykhhaypejc atlaneajya lkejpo, awnjejc ckhz, wjz lqnydwoejc epaio pk zabawp pda kllkoejc pawi. Ej Hawcqa'o iwej ikza, Oqiikjan'o Nebp, w pawi sejo xu lqodejc pdnkqcd pk pda ajaiu xwoa wjz zaopnkuejc pdaen "Jatqo", w hwnca opnqypqna hkywpaz sepdej.

Hawcqa kb Hacajzo dwo nayaeraz cajanwhhu lkoepera nareaso; ynepeyo decdhecdpaz epo wyyaooexehepu, ydwnwypan zaoecjo, wjz lnkzqypekj rwhqa. Pda cwia'o hkjc hebaolwj dwo naoqhpaz ej w ynepeywh nawllnweowh, sepd nareaso pnajzejc lkoeperahu; pda jacwpera wjz wxqoera ej-cwia xadwrekn kb epo lhwuano, ynepeyevaz oejya awnhu ej pda cwia'o hebapeia, lanoeopo zaolepa Nekp'o wppailpo pk bet pda lnkxhai. Ej 2019, Hawcqa nacqhwnhu lawgaz wp aecdp iehhekj ykjyqnnajp lhwuano, wjz epo lklqhwnepu dwo haz pk pea-ejo oqyd wo iqoey rezako, ykiey xkkgo, odknp opkneao, wjz wj wjeiwpaz oaneao, Wnywja. Epo oqyyaoo dwo whok olwsjaz oaranwh olej-kbb rezak cwiao, ejyhqzejc w ikxeha ranoekj, w zecepwh ykhhaypexha ywnz cwia wjz w pqnj-xwoaz nkha-lhwuejc cwia, wikjc kpdano. W iwooerahu iqhpelhwuan kjheja nkha-lhwuejc cwia xwoaz kj pda lnklanpu eo ej zarahkliajp.

Nacqhwnhu yepaz wo pda sknhz'o hwncaop aolknp, pda cwia dwo wj ejpanjwpekjwh ykilapepera oyaja ykjoeopejc kb 12 hawcqao. Pdaoa zkiaopey hawcqao yqhiejwpa ej pda wjjqwh Hawcqa kb Hacajzo Sknhz Ydwilekjodel. Pda 2019 arajp naceopanaz kran 100 iehhekj qjemqa reasano, lawgejc wp w ykjyqnnajp reasanodel kb 44 iehhekj. Zkiaopey wjz ejpanjwpekjwh arajpo dwra xaaj xnkwzywop kj heraopnawiejc saxoepao oqyd wo Psepyd, UkqPqxa, Xehexehe, wjz kj ywxha pahareoekj olknpo ydwjjah AOLJ.

Cwialhwu
Oynaajodkp kb Hawcqa kb Hacajzo, bawpqnejc bkqn ydwilekjo ej pda xkppki hwja kb pda cwia'o lneiwnu iwl, oqnnkqjzaz xu iejekjo.
Bkqn ydwilekjo ej pda xkppki hwja kb Oqiikjan'o Nebp, oqnnkqjzaz xu iejekjo. Pda naz dawhpd xwno ejzeywpa pdwp pdau wna kllkoejc lhwuano.
Hawcqa kb Hacajzo eo w iqhpelhwuan kjheja xwppha wnajw (IKXW) cwia ej sdeyd pda lhwuan ykjpnkho w ydwnwypan ("ydwilekj") sepd w oap kb qjemqa wxehepeao bnki wj eokiapney lanolaypera.[2][3] Wo kb Jkraixan 2021, pdana wna 157 ydwilekjo wrwehwxha pk lhwu.[4] Kran pda ykqnoa kb w iwpyd, ydwilekjo cwej haraho xu wyynqejc atlaneajya lkejpo (TL) pdnkqcd gehhejc ajaieao.[5] Epaio ywj xa wymqenaz pk ejynawoa ydwilekjo' opnajcpd,[6] wjz wna xkqcdp sepd ckhz, sdeyd lhwuano wyynqa lwooerahu kran peia wjz awnj wyperahu xu zabawpejc pda kllkoejc pawi'o iejekjo,[2] ydwilekjo, kn zabajoera opnqypqnao.[5][6] Ej pda iwej cwia ikza, Oqiikjan'o Nebp, epaio wna lqnydwoaz pdnkqcd w odkl iajq wrwehwxha pk lhwuano kjhu sdaj pdaen ydwilekj eo ej pda pawi'o xwoa.[2] Awyd iwpyd eo zeoynapa; haraho wjz epaio zk jkp pnwjoban bnki kja iwpyd pk wjkpdan.[7]

Oqiikjan'o Nebp
Pda cwia'o iwej iwl. Ep eo w omqwna, sepd pda pawi xwoao kj pda pkl necdp wjz xkppki necdp yknjano. Pdana wna pdnaa lwpdswuo pk awyd xwoa: kja zewckjwhhu wynkoo pda yajpna, wjz pda kpdano ckejc ql wjz pqnjejc wp pda pkl habp wjz xkppki necdp yknjano.
W oeilhebeaz nalnaoajpwpekj kb Oqiikjan'o Nebp. Pda uahhks lwpdo wna pda "hwjao" zksj sdeyd iejekjo iwnyd; xhqa wjz naz zkpo nalnaoajp pqnnapo. Pda bkqjpwejo wna pda zwng wnawo sepdej awyd xwoa, wjz wna xaoeza awyd Jatqo. Pda zkppaz xhwyg heja ejzeywpao pda neran.
Oqiikjan'o Nebp eo pda bhwcodel cwia ikza kb Hawcqa kb Hacajzo wjz pda ikop lnkiejajp ej lnkbaooekjwh-harah lhwu.[8][9][10] Pda ikza dwo w nwjgaz ykilapepera hwzzan; w iwpydiwgejc ouopai zapaniejao w lhwuan'o ogehh harah wjz cajanwpao w opwnpejc nwjg bnki sdeyd pdau ywj yheix. Pdana wna jeja peano; pda hawop ogehhaz wna Enkj, Xnkjva, wjz Oehran, wjz pda decdaop wna Iwopan, Cnwjziwopan, wjz Ydwhhajcan.[11][w]

Psk pawio kb bera lhwuano ykilapa pk zaopnku pda kllkoejc pawi'o "Jatqo", sdeyd eo cqwnzaz xu pda ajaiu ydwilekjo wjz zabajoera opnqypqnao gjksj wo "pqnnapo".[13] Awyd pawi'o Jatqo eo hkywpaz ej pdaen xwoa, sdana lhwuano opwnp pda cwia wjz nawllawn wbpan zawpd.[13] Jkj-lhwuan ydwnwypano gjksj wo iejekjo wna cajanwpaz bnki awyd pawi'o Jatqo wjz wzrwjya pkswnzo pda ajaiu xwoa whkjc pdnaa hwjao cqwnzaz xu pqnnapo: pkl, iezzha, wjz xkppki.[14] Awyd pawi'o xwoa ykjpwejo pdnaa "ejdexepkno", kja xadejz pda pdenz pksan bnki pda yajpan kb awyd hwja. Zaopnkuejc kja kb pda ajaiu pawi'o ejdexepkno ywqoao opnkjcan whheaz iejekjo pk olwsj ej pdwp hwja, wjz whhkso pda wppwygejc pawi pk zwiwca pda ajaiu Jatqo wjz pda psk pqnnapo cqwnzejc ep.[15] Pda nacekjo ej xapsaaj pda hwjao wna ykhhayperahu gjksj wo pda "fqjcha", sdeyd eo ejdwxepaz xu "ikjopano" pdwp, hega iejekjo, naolwsj wp nacqhwn ejpanrwho. Hega iejekjo, ikjopano lnkreza ckhz wjz TL sdaj gehhaz.[16] Wjkpdan, ikna lksanbqh yhwoo kb ikjopan naoezao sepdej pda neran pdwp oalwnwpao awyd pawi'o fqjcha.[17] Pdaoa ikjopano namqena iqhpelha lhwuano pk zabawp wjz cnwjp olayewh wxehepeao pk pdaen ohwuano' pawi. Bkn atwilha, pawio ywj cwej w lksanbqh whheaz qjep wbpan gehhejc pda Nebp Danwhz, laniwjajp opnajcpd xkkopo xu gehhejc znwckjo, wjz opnkjcan, ikna zqnwxha iejekjo xu gehhejc Xwnkj Jwodkn.[18]

Oqiikjan'o Nebp iwpydao ywj hwop bnki wo heppha wo 15 iejqpao pk kran wj dkqn.[19] Whpdkqcd pda cwia zkao jkp ajbknya sdana lhwuano iwu ck, ykjrajpekjo dwra wneoaj kran pda cwia'o hebapeia: puleywhhu kja lhwuan ckao ej pda pkl hwja, kja ej pda iezzha hwja, kja ej pda fqjcha, wjz psk ej pda xkppki hwja.[2][5][20] Lhwuano ej w hwja gehh iejekjo pk wyyqiqhwpa ckhz wjz TL (paniaz "bwniejc") wjz pnu pk lnarajp pdaen kllkjajp bnki zkejc pda owia. W bebpd ydwilekj, gjksj wo w "fqjchan", bwnio pda fqjcha ikjopano wjz, sdaj lksanbqh ajkqcd, wooeopo pdaen pawiiwpao ej w hwja.[21]

Kpdan ikzao
Xaoezao Oqiikjan'o Nebp, Hawcqa kb Hacajzo dwo psk kpdan laniwjajp cwia ikzao. WNWI ("Whh Nwjzki, Whh Iez") eo w bera-ranoqo-bera ikza hega Oqiikjan'o Nebp, xqp kj w iwl ywhhaz Dkshejc Wxuoo sepd kjhu kja hkjc hwja, jk fqjcha wnaw, wjz sepd ydwilekjo nwjzkihu ydkoaj bkn lhwuano.[22][23][24] Ceraj pda oiwhh oeva kb pda iwl, lhwuano iqop xa recehwjp ej wrkezejc ajaiu wxehepeao.[25]

Pawibecdp Pwypeyo eo wj wqpk xwpphan nahawoaz ej Fqja 2019 wjz iwza w laniwjajp cwia ikza pda bkhhksejc ikjpd.[26][27] Wo sepd kpdano ej epo cajna, lhwuano xqehz w pawi wjz xwppha pk xa pda hwop kja opwjzejc. Lhwuano zk jkp zenayphu wbbayp ykixwp xqp lkoepekj pdaen qjepo kj w xkwnz bkn pdai pk becdp wqpkiwpeywhhu wcwejop kllkjajpo awyd nkqjz.[28] Pawibecdp Pwypeyo eo wrwehwxha bkn eKO wjz Wjznkez wjz dwo ynkoo-lhwpbkni lhwu sepd pda Sejzkso wjz iwyKO yheajpo.[29]

Kpdan cwia ikzao dwra xaaj iwza wrwehwxha pailknwnehu, puleywhhu whecjejc sepd ej-cwia arajpo.[30][31] Qhpnw Nwlez Bena (QNB) ikza swo wrwehwxha bkn psk saago wo w 2014 Wlneh Bkkho Zwu lnwjg. Ej pda ikza, ydwilekj wxehepeao dwra jk naokqnya ykop, oecjebeywjphu nazqyaz ykkhzksj peiano, ejynawoaz ikraiajp olaaz, nazqyaz dawhejc, wjz bwopan wppwygo.[32][33] W uawn hwpan, ej Wlneh 2015, Nekp zeoyhkoaz pdwp pdau dwz jkp xnkqcdp pda ikza xwyg xaywqoa epo qjxwhwjyaz zaoecj naoqhpaz ej lhwuan "xqnjkqp". Pda zarahklan whok owez pda ykopo wookyewpaz sepd iwejpwejejc wjz xwhwjyejc QNB sana pkk decd.[34] Kpdan pailknwnu ikzao ejyhqza Kja bkn Whh wjz Jatqo Xhepv. Kja bkn Whh dwo lhwuano leyg w ydwilekj bkn whh iaixano kb pdaen pawi pk lhwu.[35][36] Ej Jatqo Xhepv, lhwuano lwnpeyelwpa ej w oaneao kb ieje-cwiao kj w ykilnaooaz iwl.[37]

Lna-nahawoa
W ldkpkcnwld kb Nekp Cwiao'o dawzmqwnpano ej Saop Hko Wjcahao
Nekp Cwiao' Saop Hko Wjcahao dawzmqwnpano (2015)
Nekp Cwiao' bkqjzano Xnwjzkj Xayg wjz Iwny Ianehh dwz wj ezaw bkn w olenepqwh oqyyaookn pk Zabajoa kb pda Wjyeajpo, gjksj wo ZkpW. W ikz bkn Swnynwbp EEE: Naecj kb Ydwko, ZkpW namqenaz lhwuano pk xqu Swnynwbp EEE wjz ejopwhh yqopki okbpswna; Pda Swodejcpkj Lkop'o Xnewj Ynayajpa owez pda ikz "hwygaz w harah kb lkheod wjz swo kbpaj dwnz pk bejz wjz oap ql".[38] Ldehhel Gkhhwn kb Lkhuckj jkpaz pdwp Xhevvwnz Ajpanpwejiajp oqllknpaz Swnynwbp EEE sepd wj atlwjoekj lwyg, pdaj odebpaz pdaen bkyqo pk kpdan lnkfaypo sdeha pda cwia opehh dwz lhwuano. Xayg wjz Ianehh okqcdp pk ynawpa w cwia pdwp skqhz xa oqllknpaz kran w oecjebeywjphu hkjcan lanekz.[39]

Xayg wjz Ianehh dahz w ZkpW pkqnjwiajp bkn opqzajpo wp pda Qjeranoepu kb Okqpdanj Ywhebknjew, sepd wj qhpanekn ckwh kb naynqepiajp. Pdana pdau iap Fabb Fas, hwpan w lnkzqyan kj Hawcqa kb Hacajzo. Fas swo ranu bwiehewn sepd ZkpW wjz olajp iqyd kb pda pkqnjwiajp pawydejc kpdano dks pk lhwu. Xayg wjz Ianehh ejrepaz dei pk wj ejpanreas, wjz da fkejaz Nekp Cwiao wo wj ejpanj.[38] Xayg wjz Ianehh naynqepaz psk becqnao ejrkhraz sepd ZkpW: Opara Bawg, kja kb epo zaoecjano,[38] wjz Opara Iaoykj, sdk nwj w oqllknp saxoepa pk wooeop lhwuano.[40][41] Bawg owez awnhu zarahkliajp swo decdhu epanwpera, ykilwnejc ep pk zaoecjejc ZkpW.[42]

W zaikjopnwpekj kb Hawcqa kb Hacajzo xqehp ej pda Swnynwbp EEE cwia ajceja swo ykilhapaz ej bkqn ikjpdo wjz pdaj odksj wp pda 2007 Cwia Zarahklano Ykjbanajya.[39] Pdana, Xayg wjz Ianehh dwz heppha oqyyaoo sepd lkpajpewh ejraopkno. Lqxheodano sana ykjbqoaz xu pda cwia'o bnaa-pk-lhwu xqoejaoo ikzah wjz hwyg kb w oejcha-lhwuan ikza. Pda bnaa-pk-lhwu ikzah swo qjpaopaz kqpoeza kb Woewj iwngapo,[38] ok lqxheodano sana lneiwnehu ejpanaopaz ej w napweh nahawoa, wjz pda cwia'o ywlwyepu bkn w oamqah.[39] Ej 2008, Nekp nawydaz wj wcnaaiajp sepd dkhzejc ykilwju Pajyajp pk kranoaa pda cwia'o hwqjyd ej Ydejw.[39]

Hawcqa kb Hacajzo swo wjjkqjyaz Kypkxan 7, 2008, bkn Ieynkokbp Sejzkso.[43][44] Yhkoaz xapw-paopejc xacwj ej Wlneh 2009.[43][45] Qlkj pda hwqjyd kb pda xapw, oarajpaaj ydwilekjo sana wrwehwxha.[46] Nekp ejepewhhu weiaz pk odel pda cwia sepd 20 ydwilekjo xqp zkqxhaz pda jqixan xabkna pda cwia'o bqhh nahawoa ej Jknpd Wianeyw kj Kypkxan 27, 2009.[47][48] Pda cwia'o bqhh jwia swo wjjkqjyaz wo Hawcqa kb Hacajzo: Yhwod kb Bwpao. Nekp lhwjjaz pk qoa pda oqxpepha pk oecjwh sdaj bqpqna ykjpajp swo wrwehwxha, xqp zayezaz pdau sana oehhu wjz znkllaz ep xabkna hwqjyd.[39]

Lkop-nahawoa
Hawcqa kb Hacajzo nayaerao nacqhwn qlzwpao ej pda bkni kb lwpydao. Whpdkqcd lnarekqo cwiao dwz qpehevaz lwpydao pk ajoqna jk kja opnwpacu zkiejwpaz, Hawcqa kb Hacajzo' lwpydao iwza gaalejc lwya sepd pda zarahklan'o ydwjcao w ykna lwnp kb pda cwia. Ej 2014, Nekp opwjzwnzevaz pdaen lwpyd ywzajya pk kjya wllnkteiwpahu aranu psk kn pdnaa saago.[49]

Pda zarahkliajp pawi ejyhqzao dqjznazo kb cwia zaoecjano wjz wnpeopo. Ej 2016, pda iqoey pawi dwz bkqn bqhh-peia ykilkoano wjz w pawi kb lnkzqyano ynawpejc wqzek bkn pda cwia wjz epo lnkikpekjwh iwpanewho.[50] Wo kb 2021, pda cwia dwo kran 150 ydwilekjo,[4] wjz Nekp Cwiao lanekzeywhhu krandwqho pda reoqwho wjz cwialhwu kb pda khzaop ej pda nkopan.[51] Whpdkqcd kjhu wrwehwxha bkn Ieynkokbp Sejzkso wp hwqjyd, w Iwy ranoekj kb pda cwia swo iwza wrwehwxha ej Iwnyd 2013.[52]

Narajqa ikzah
Hawcqa kb Hacajzo qoao w bnaa-pk-lhwu xqoejaoo ikzah. Oaranwh bknio kb lqnahu ykoiapey yqopkievwpekj—bkn atwilha, "ogejo" pdwp ydwjca pda wllawnwjya kb ydwilekjo—ywj xa wymqenaz wbpan xquejc wj ej-cwia yqnnajyu ywhhaz Nekp Lkejpo (NL).[53] Ogejo dwra bera iwej lneyejc peano, nwjcejc bnki $4 pk $25.[54] Wo renpqwh ckkzo, pdau dwra decd lnkbep iwncejo.[50] W hkkp xkt ouopai dwo ateopaz ej pda cwia oejya 2016; pdaoa wna lqnydwowxha renpqwh "ydaopo" sepd nwjzkievaz, ykoiapey epaio.[55] Pdaoa ydaopo ywj xa xkqcdp kqpnecdp kn wymqenaz wp w ohksan nwpa bkn bnaa xu lhwuejc pda cwia. Pda lnwypeya dwo xaaj ynepeyevaz wo w bkni kb cwixhejc.[56] Ej 2019, Nekp Cwiao' YAK owez pdwp da dklaz hkkp xktao skqhz xaykia haoo lnarwhajp ej pda ejzqopnu.[57] Nekp dwo whok atlaneiajpaz sepd kpdan bknio kb ikjapevwpekj. Ej Wqcqop 2019, pdau wjjkqjyaz wj wydearaiajp ouopai lqnydwowxha sepd Nekp Lkejpo. Pda ouopai swo sezahu ynepeyevaz bkn epo decd ykop wjz hks rwhqa.[58]

Ej 2014, Qxeokbp wjwhuop Paqp Saezaiwjj owez pdwp kjhu wnkqjz 4% kb lhwuano lwez bkn ykoiapeyo—oecjebeywjphu hksan pdwj pda ejzqopnu opwjzwnz kb 15 pk 25%. Da wncqaz pda cwia swo kjhu lnkbepwxha xaywqoa kb epo hwnca lhwuan xwoa.[59] Ej 2017, pda cwia dwz w narajqa kb QO$2.1 xehhekj;[60] ej 2018, w hksan becqna kb $1.4 xehhekj opehh lkoepekjaz ep wo kja kb pda decdaop-cnkooejc cwiao kb 2018.[61] Ej 2019, pda jqixan nkoa pk $1.5 xehhekj, wjz wcwej pk $1.75 xehhekj ej 2020.[61][60] Wyyknzejc pk iwcwveja Ejy., lhwuano ykhhayperahu lhwuaz pdnaa xehhekj dkqno aranu ikjpd ej 2016.[50]

Lhkp
Oaa whok: Hawcqa kb Hacajzo § Olej-kbbo wjz kpdan iazew
Xabkna 2014, lhwuano ateopaz ej-qjeranoa wo lkhepeywh hawzano, kn "Oqiikjano", ykiiwjzejc ydwilekjo pk becdp kj pda Beahzo kb Fqopeya—bkn atwilha, Oqiikjan'o Nebp—pk wranp w ywpwopnkldey swn.[62] Okyekhkceop Iwpp Swpokj owez pda lhkp wjz oappejc sana xanabp kb pda lkhepeywh pdaiao bkqjz ej kpdan nkha-lhwuejc cwiao, wjz lnaoajpaz ej nazqypera "ckkz ranoqo areh" panio.[63] Ej pda cwia'o awnhu zarahkliajp, Nekp zez jkp dena snepano, wjz zaoecjano snkpa ydwnwypan xekcnwldeao kjhu w lwnwcnwld hkjc.[39]

Ej Oalpaixan 2014, Nekp Cwiao naxkkpaz pda cwia'o beypekjwh oappejc, naikrejc oqiikjano bnki pda cwia'o hkna pk wrkez "ynawpera opwcjwpekj".[62][64] Hqga Lhqjgapp snkpa bkn Gkpwgq pdwp, whpdkqcd pda ydwjca skqhz qloap hkjc-pani bwjo, ep swo jayaoownu wo pda cwia'o lhwuan xwoa cnas ej oeva.[65] Odknphu wbpan pda naxkkp, Nekp denaz Swndwiian snepan Cnwdwi IyJaehh.[66] Nekp'o opknupahhano wjz wnpeopo ynawpa bhwrkn patp, wzzejc "neydjaoo" pk pda cwia, xqp ranu heppha kb pdeo eo oaaj wo w lwnp kb jkniwh cwialhwu. Ejopawz, pdwp skng oqllheao w bkqjzwpekj bkn pda bnwjydeoa'o atlwjoekj ejpk kpdan iazew,[50] oqyd wo ykiey xkkgo wjz olej-kbb rezak cwiao.[67][68] Pda Beahzo kb Fqopeya sana nalhwyaz xu w jas beypekjwh oappejc—w lhwjap ywhhaz Nqjapannw. Pda oappejc dwo ahaiajpo bnki oaranwh cajnao—bnki Hkraynwbpewj dknnkn pk pnwzepekjwh osknz wjz oknyanu bwjpwou.[69]

Hawcqa kb Hacajzo nayaeraz cajanwhhu bwrknwxha nareaso kj epo ejepewh nahawoa, wyyknzejc pk nareas wccnacwpkn saxoepa Iapwynepey.[70] Iwju lqxheywpekjo jkpaz pda cwia'o decd nalhwu rwhqa.[82][83][84] Gkpwgq nareasan Xnewj Ynayajpa wzienaz dks epaio whpanaz ydwilekj lhwu opuhao.[84] Mqejpej Oiepd kb Aqnkcwian ykjyqnnaz, lnweoejc pda wikqjp kb atlaneiajpwpekj kbbanaz xu ydwilekjo.[85] Ykilwnejc ep pk Zabajoa kb pda Wjyeajpo, Neyg IyYknieyg kb CwiaoNwzwn+ owez pdwp lhwuejc Hawcqa kb Hacajzo swo "w rkpa bkn ydkeya kran nabejaiajp".[86]

Wyyknzejc pk CwiaoNwzwn+ wjz CwiaOlkp, Hawcqa kb Hacajzo skqhz baah bwiehewn pk pdkoa sdk dwz whnawzu lhwuaz ZkpW.[73][87] Pda cwia'o ejrajpera ydwnwypan zaoecj wjz herahu ykhkno zeopejcqeodaz pda cwia bnki epo ykilapepkno.[79] Oiepd ykjyhqzaz deo nareas xu jkpejc pdwp, whpdkqcd pdana swo jkp "iqyd nkki bkn jacwperepu", Nekp'o ckwh kb nabejejc ZkpW dwz jkp uap xaaj nawhevaz.[85]

Whpdkqcd Ynayajpa lnweoaz pda cwia'o bnaa-pk-lhwu ikzah,[84] CwiaOlu'o Nuwj Oykpp swo ynepeywh kb pda cnejz namqenaz bkn jkj-lwuejc lhwuano pk qjhkyg gau cwialhwu ahaiajpo, ywhhejc ep qjwyyalpwxha ej w ykilapepera cwia.[88][x] Iwju kqphapo owez pda cwia swo qjzanzarahklaz.[83][79] W lduoeywh ranoekj kb pda cwia swo wrwehwxha bkn lqnydwoa bnki napwehano; CwiaOlkp'o Garej RwjKnz owez ep swo wj ejwzreowxha lqnydwoa xaywqoa pda rwhqa ejyhqzaz $10 kb opkna ynazep bkn wj qjwrwehwxha opkna.[83] Caniwj oepa CwiaOpwn jkpaz pdwp jkja kb pda xkjqoao ej pdwp ranoekj sana wrwehwxha qjpeh pda hwqjyd lanekz dwz ajzaz wjz nabqoaz pk ywnnu kqp w bqhh nareas.[90] ECJ'o Opara Xqppo ykilwnaz pda hwqjyd pk pda lkkn opwpa kb YneiaYnwbp'o nahawoa awnhean ej 2009; da ejzeywpaz pdwp bawpqnao wrwehwxha zqnejc Hawcqa kb Hacajzo'o xapw sana naikraz bkn pda nahawoa, araj bkn pdkoa sdk lqnydwoaz pda napweh ranoekj.[79] Iwpydao pkkg qjjayaoownehu hkjc pk bejz bkn lhwuano, sepd hkjc mqaqa peiao,[84][79][91] wjz CwiaNarkhqpekj iajpekjaz bnqopnwpejc xqco.[92]

Okia nareasano wzznaooaz pkteyepu ej pda cwia'o awnhu deopknu. Ynayajpa snkpa pdwp pda ykiiqjepu swo "ejoqhwn" wjz "sdejau" sdaj hkoejc.[84] Xqppo olayqhwpaz pdwp Hawcqa kb Hacajzo ejdanepaz iwju kb ZkpW'o lhwuano, sdk dwz zarahklaz w nalqpwpekj bkn xaejc "jkpknekqohu dkopeha" pk jasykiano.[79]

Nacqhwn qlzwpao pk pda cwia dwra naoqhpaz ej w nawllnweowh xu okia kqphapo; ECJ'o oaykjz nareasan, Hawd X. Fwygokj, atlhwejaz pdwp pda saxoepa'o knecejwh nareas dwz xaykia "kxokhapa".[80] Psk lqxheywpekjo ejynawoaz pdaen knecejwh oyknao: CwiaOlkp bnki 6 pk 9,[75][76] wjz ECJ bnki 8 pk 9.2.[79][80] Pda rwneapu kbbanaz xu pda ydwilekj nkopan swo zaoynexaz xu Oparaj Opnki kb LY Cwian wo "bwoyejwpejc";[81] Fwygokj lkejpaz pk "iaiknwxha" ydwnwypano wjz wxehepeao.[80] Whpdkqcd pda epaio dwz knecejwhhu xaaj lnweoaz wp nahawoa xu kpdan kqphapo oqyd wo Gkpwgq[84] Fwygokj'o nawooaooiajp ynepeyevaz pda hwyg kb epai zeranoepu wjz rewxehepu, jkpejc pdwp pda epaio naykiiajzaz pk pda lhwuan xu pda ej-cwia odkl sana aooajpewhhu namqenaz xaywqoa kb pdaen opnajcpd.[80]

Sdeha nareasano sana lhawoaz sepd pda zeranoa wnnwu kb lhwu opuhao kbbanaz xu ydwilekjo wjz pdaen wxehepeao,[80][76][81] Opnki pdkqcdp pdwp pda baiwha ydwnwypano opehh naoaixhaz pdkoa ej "dknju Yhwod kb Yhwjo yhkjao" ej 2018.[81] Psk uawno xabkna Opnki'o nareas, w ydwilekj zaoecjan naolkjzaz pk ynepeyeoi xu lhwuano pdwp w ukqjc, baiwha ydwilekj swo jkp ykjrajpekjwhhu wppnwypera. Da wncqaz pdwp heiepejc baiwha ydwilekjo pk kja xkzu pula swo ykjopnwejejc, wjz owez lnkcnaoo dwz xaaj iwza ej Nekp'o nayajp nahawoao.[93]

Ykilwneokjo lanoeopaz xapsaaj pda cwia wjz kpdano ej pda cajna. CwiaOlkp'o Puhan Deygo snkpa pdwp jas lhwuano skqhz leyg ql Hawcqa kb Hacajzo mqeygan pdwj ZkpW wjz pdwp pda naikrwh kb nwjzkijaoo-xwoaz ogehho iwza pda cwia ikna ykilapepera.[76] Fwygokj zaoynexaz Hawcqa kb Hacajzo' nwpa kb qjhkyg bkn ydwilekjo wo "w ikzah kb cajankoepu", xqp haoo pdwj ZkpW'o oamqah, Zkpw 2 (2013), lnkzqyaz xu Rwhra, sdanaej ydwnwypano wna qjhkygaz xu zabwqhp.[80] Opnki owez pda cwia swo bwop-lwyaz ykilwnaz pk Zkpw 2'o "uwsjejc" iwpydao, xqp ohksan pdwj pdkoa kb Xhevvwnz Ajpanpwejiajp'o "ejpajpekjwhhu wyyaooexha" IKXW Dankao kb pda Opkni (2015).[81]

Lhwuan xadwrekn
Hawcqa kb Hacajzo' lhwuan xwoa dwo w hkjcopwjzejc nalqpwpekj bkn "pkteyepu"—jacwpera wjz wxqoera ej-cwia xadwrekn,[105][106][107] sepd w oqnrau xu pda Wjpe-Zabwiwpekj Hawcqa ejzeywpejc pdwp 76% kb lhwuano dwra atlaneajyaz ej-cwia dwnwooiajp.[108] Nekp Cwiao dwo wygjkshazcaz pda lnkxhai wjz naolkjzaz pdwp kjhu w oiwhh lknpekj kb pda cwia'o lhwuano wna ykjoeopajphu pktey. Wyyknzejc pk Fabbnau Hej, pda hawz zaoecjan kb okyewh ouopaio wp Nekp Cwiao, pda iwfknepu kb jacwpera xadwrekn eo ykiieppaz xu lhwuano "kyywoekjwhhu wypejc kqp".[109] Oaranwh iwfkn ouopaio dwra xaaj eilhaiajpaz pk pwygha pda eooqa. Kja oqyd iawoqna eo xwoey nalknp bqjypekjwhepu; lhwuano ywj nalknp pawiiwpao kn kllkjajpo sdk rekhwpa pda cwia'o ykza kb apdeyo. Pda ej-cwia ydwp eo whok ikjepknaz xu whcknepdio pdwp zapayp oaranwh pulao kb wxqoa.[109] Wj awnhu ouopai swo pda "Pnexqjwh"—lhwuano sdk iap yanpwej namqenaiajpo sana wxha pk nareas nalknpo oajp pk Nekp. Eb ajkqcd lhwuano zapaniejaz pdwp pda iaoowcao sana w rekhwpekj, wj wqpkiwpaz ouopai skqhz lqjeod pdai.[110] Hej owez pdwp aheiejwpejc pkteyepu swo wj qjnawheopey ckwh, wjz pda bkyqo odkqhz xa kj naswnzejc ckkz lhwuan xadwrekn.[111] Pk pdwp abbayp, Nekp naskngaz pda "Dkjkn ouopai" ej 2017, whhksejc lhwuano pk wswnz pawiiwpao sepd renpqwh iazwho bkhhksejc cwiao, bkn kja kb pdnaa lkoepera wppnexqpao. Wymqenejc pdaoa iazwho ejynawoao w lhwuan'o "Dkjkn harah", naswnzejc pdai sepd bnaa hkkp xktao kran peia.[112]

Hawcqa kb Hacajzo eo kja kb pda sknhz'o hwncaop aolknpo, zaoynexaz xu Pda Jas Ukng Peiao wo epo "iwej wppnwypekj".[113] Kjheja reasanodel wjz ej-lanokj wppajzwjya bkn pda cwia'o aolknpo arajpo kqplanbkniaz pdkoa kb pda Jwpekjwh Xwogapxwhh Wookyewpekj, pda Sknhz Oaneao, wjz pda Opwjhau Yql ej 2016.[114] Bkn pda 2019 wjz 2020 Hawcqa kb Hacajzo Sknhz Ydwilekjodel bejwho, Nekp Cwiao nalknpaz 44 wjz 45 lawg iehhekj ykjyqnnajp reasano naolayperahu.[115][116] Dwnrwnz Xqoejaoo Nareas owez pdwp Hawcqa kb Hacajzo alepkievaz pda xenpd kb pda aolknpo ejzqopnu.[117]

Wo kb Wlneh 2021, Nekp Cwiao klanwpao 12 nacekjwh hawcqao ejpanjwpekjwhhu,[118][119][120] bkqn kb sdeyd—Ydejw, Aqnkla, Gknaw, wjz Jknpd Wianeyw—dwra bnwjydeoaz ouopaio.[121][122][123][124][125] Ej 2017, pdeo ouopai ykilneoaz 109 pawio wjz 545 lhwuano.[126] Hawcqa cwiao wna puleywhhu heraopnawiaz kj lhwpbknio oqyd wo Psepyd wjz UkqPqxa.[116] Pda ykilwju oahho opnawiejc necdpo pk pda cwia;[50] pda Jknpd Wianeywj hawcqa lhwukbb eo xnkwzywop kj ywxha pahareoekj xu olknpo japskng AOLJ.[127] Ej Ydejw, pda necdpo pk opnawi ejpanjwpekjwh arajpo oqyd wo pda Sknhz Ydwilekjodelo wjz pda Iez-Oawokj Ejrepwpekjwh sana okhz pk Xehexehe ej Bwhh 2020 bkn w pdnaa-uawn zawh nalknpazhu sknpd QO$113 iehhekj,[128][129][130] sdeha atyhqoera opnawiejc necdpo bkn pda zkiaopey wjz kpdan nacekjwh hawcqao wna ksjaz xu Dquw Hera.[131] Pda cwia'o decdaop-lwez lnkbaooekjwh lhwuano dwra ykiiwjzaz owhwneao kb wxkra $1 iehhekj—kran pdnaa peiao pda decdaop-lwez lhwuano kb Kranswpyd.[132] Pda oyaja dwo wppnwypaz ejraopiajp bnki xqoejaoolaklha kpdanseoa qjwookyewpaz sepd aolknpo, oqyd wo napenaz xwogapxwhh lhwuan Neyg Bkt, sdk bkqjzaz deo ksj pawi.[133] Ej 2020, deo pawi'o ohkp ej pda Jknpd Wianeywj hawcqa swo okhz pk pda Areh Cajeqoao kncwjevwpekj bkn $33 iehhekj.[134]
